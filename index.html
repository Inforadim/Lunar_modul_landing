Lunar Module · Touch Control

EAGLE

Land an Apollo-class Lunar Module by swiping the screen. Real lunar gravity, real inertia, limited fuel.

Swipe up increase descent-engine throttle
Swipe down decrease throttle
Swipe left tilt the module left
Swipe right tilt the module right
Set down gently: under 2 m/s down, near-zero drift, nearly level, on the lit pad.
Houston radio chatter plays in the background and fades out near the surface. Toggle it with the COMMS button.
Landing zone
Keep one finger on the screen and drag: up/down controls thrust, left/right tilts the module. You can lift and re-drag as often as you like.
